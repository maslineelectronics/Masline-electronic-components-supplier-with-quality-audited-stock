## Multi-Level Quality Inspection and Testing

To maintain the highest quality control, Masline uses a detailed, multi-layer inspection process:

### 1. Visual and Surface Inspection
Each component is examined for surface irregularities, markings, resealed packaging, or physical damage.

### 2. Microscopic Analysis
Micro-level inspection identifies solder marks, resurfacing, or other signs of tampering.

### 3. Electrical Performance Testing
Electronic components such as ICs, power devices, and sensors undergo electrical validation to ensure they meet datasheet specifications.

### 4. X-Ray and Internal Structure Verification
For high-reliability applications, X-ray imaging is performed to inspect internal die structure and bond-wire integrity.

### 5. Traceability and Documentation Review
Masline verifies manufacturer labels, date codes, and production batches to ensure authentic origin.

### 6. Environmental and Compliance Checks
RoHS, REACH, and global environmental regulatory documents are validated for full compliance.

This multi-step approach ensures safe, dependable sourcing for complex engineering and production environments.
